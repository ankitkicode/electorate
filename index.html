VISION

We envision a resilient bilateral corridor where governments, enterprises, and institutions collaborate seamlessly to strengthen prosperity, enhance global competitiveness, and promote long-term stability for the people and industries of Mexico and India.

See More

MISSION

The mission of the MIBC is to serve as a premier platform for strengthening bilateral economic relations through diplomacy, strategic engagement, and high-quality trade and investment facilitation.

See More

Goal

Our goal is to strengthen the Mexico–India economic partnership by creating a structured, credible, and future-oriented platform that advances trade, investment, and strategic cooperation between the two countries.

See More

Why the INDIA–MEXICO Corridor ?

Rising India–Mexico Trade

Bilateral trade now exceeds USD 10 billion, driven by strong growth in automotive, pharma, IT, energy, and agriculture. Both nations are expanding investments and diversifying supply chains.

Enhancing Bilateral Cooperation

Mexico and India continue strengthening cooperation through strategic dialogues, economic missions, and collaborative initiatives across innovation, technology, and trade sectors.
